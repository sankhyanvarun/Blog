My Journey into AI Automation

The Spark of an Idea

Have you ever found yourself endlessly scrolling through a clunky website, hunting for a single piece of information, and thinking, "There has to be a better way"? As a fourth-year engineering student specializing in AI and Machine Learning, that question sparked a fascination that has defined my academic journey. I saw a world full of repetitive, time-consuming tasks and realized that AI could be the key to unlocking a more efficient future for everyone. This isn't just about futuristic robots; it's about practical solutions for everyday problems. And I decided to build one myself.

The Problem: The Campus Information Maze

Every college student knows the struggle. You need to know the deadline for course registration, the date of the next big campus event, or the contact info for a specific department. The first stop is usually the college website—a digital labyrinth of menus, outdated pages, and broken links. This process is inefficient, frustrating, and a huge time sink for students, parents, and staff alike.

The Solution: An AI-Powered College Chatbot

To tackle this problem head-on, I developed a College Chatbot. Imagine a single point of contact, available 24/7, that holds all the knowledge of the college website and more. Instead of searching, you simply ask a question in plain English, and you get an answer in seconds.

A user asking the chatbot for fees.

A Look Under the Hood: The Tech Stack

For those interested in the technical details, this chatbot is powered by some exciting technology:

  • The Brain: The core of the chatbot is the Mistral 7B model, a powerful Large Language Model.
  • The Technique: I used RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to ensure the bot provides factual, context-specific answers from a verified knowledge base.
  • The Performance: The result is a highly responsive tool that handles most queries in under one to two minutes.

Why It's a Game-Changer

The chatbot doesn't just replicate the website; it vastly improves upon it by providing instant, conversational support 24/7. It turns a frustrating search into a simple conversation.
